Ray K. Yang’s Blog

Welcome to my blog! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in {Strategy} @ University of California, Riverside.

My research concerns the interplay between corporate strategy and the social structure of the market. For example, I study how mergers and acquisitions (M&As) change a firm's identity in relation to its peers and the inter-firm network structure, triggering the stock market's reactions.

I am a data science hobbyist. I like applying machine learning in strategy. As we envision and observe how things connect, our minds naturally turn to what makes them tick. With me, knowledge production/application is an infinite loop of (causal) reasoning, modeling, and predictions.
